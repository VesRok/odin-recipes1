Low-fat high-protein bolognese

Description

Want a low fat, high protein but a still JUICY lunch or dinner ?
You will love this wine infused creamy milk bolognese mixed with protein pasta and topped with some parsley and ofcourse hard cheese grana padano

Ingredients 5portions

  • 1 kg low-fat pork-beef rind
  • 3 large onions - diced
  • 3 large carrots - diced
  • 400 g protein pasta
  • 500 ml pureed tomatoes
  • 200 ml red wine Stron GEMAY preffered
  • 100 ml low-fat milk
  • 1 cube of beef stock
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • salt, pepper, paprika, bay leaves

Instructions

  1. Sautee diced onions and diced carrots on a bit of oil in a pan untill onion is "transparent".
  2. Add the meat rind and mix it in, cook untill water from the meat evaporates and the meat becomes brownish.
  3. Salt and Pepper on the meat, add diced garlic, MIX for 1 minute.
  4. Pour wine, mix untill alcohol evaporates (alcohol smell significantly reduces)
  5. Pour milk, mix
  6. Pour tomatoe puree, add paprika, beef stock, mix
  7. If too dense, add water
  8. Add 3 bay leaves, reduce heat and on low to medium heat cook for atleast 1hour, prefferably 3hours for best taste
  9. Cook the pasta in salty water, cook Al dente.
    Leave some pasta water for later if you need to make the bolognese less dense.
  10. Serve with hard cheese grated on and optionaly some parsley for freshness
