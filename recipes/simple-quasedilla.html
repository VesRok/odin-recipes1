Simple ham-cheese egg quasedilla

Description

QUICK yet full of protein fast simple quasedilla, 2 tortillas and some eggs,meat and cheese.
Sometimes the simpler the better

Ingredients

  • 2 whole grain tortillas (whole grain makes you more full)
  • ham,cheese
  • tomatoe puree
  • 2eggs + 2-3 egg whites
  • salt, pepper, oregano

Instructions

  1. Mix eggs and egg whites, pour in to the pan
  2. Salt and pepper the mixture and on top put a tortilla
  3. when cooked through and stuck on the tortilla, FLIP it
  4. apply tomatoe puree,salt the puree, add oregano, ham,cheese and on top another tortilla
  5. FLIP, and cook on the otherside on low-to medium heat untill tortilla gets crispy and cheese melts

    6. Enjoy the low calorie "PIZZA"
